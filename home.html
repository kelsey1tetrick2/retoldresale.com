What We Offer

One boutique. Three ways to support sustainable fashion.

Shop Retold

Browse racks of gently-loved fashion curated by independent sellers. Discover vintage finds, modern staples, and one-of-a-kind pieces.

Rent a Booth

Sell through our boutique without the overhead of your own storefront. Own your inventory, set your prices, keep 65% of every sale. From $35/month.

Alterations

Hemming, mending, button replacements, zippers, resizing, and vintage restoration. Give a piece the ending it deserves — or a whole new beginning.

For Sellers

Rent a booth in five simple steps.

01

Rent Your Booth

Schedule your week and purchase your booth rental. Every story needs a stage — this is yours.

02

Style Your Space

Saturday, 8–10 am: tag your items, hang your pieces, and set up your signs. Your chapter opens for the week.

03

We Sell For You

We keep your booth clean, tidy, and selling. We can even run discounts throughout the week to keep the plot moving.

04

Collect Your Earnings

65% of every sale is yours. Restock at any time during the week to keep the story fresh.

05

Take Down

Friday, you come grab your pieces — or for a small fee, we'll handle the final edit so you don't have to.
