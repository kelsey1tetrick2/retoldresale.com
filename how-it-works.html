Questions

Things sellers ask us.

Do I have to be at the store to sell?
No. Once your rack is stocked and styled, our staff handles the floor and sales. You can pop in to refresh inventory whenever you'd like, but you're never required to be present.
Who owns the merchandise on my rack?
You do. Always. Unlike consignment stores that take ownership, you retain 100% ownership of everything on your rack. Retold simply provides the space and sales infrastructure.
How does the commission work?
You keep 65% of the sale price. Retold retains 30%. This is separate from your monthly rack rental fee. Earnings are tracked in real-time through our POS system and paid out on a regular schedule.
What can I sell?
Clothing, accessories, shoes, and fashion items. All merchandise must be clean and gently used. We welcome vintage finds, contemporary resale, handmade pieces, and curated collections. We'll chat about fit during your intro call.
What if items don't sell?
You can rotate inventory anytime. Swap slow movers for fresh pieces, adjust prices, or try new styling approaches. We'll also share data on what's trending to help you make smarter restocking decisions.
Can I sell online too?
Absolutely. You're free to run your own online resale presence independently. We also offer optional promotional add-ons that include social media features and online visibility for your rack.
